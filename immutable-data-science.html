# Immutable Data Science

We are the developers of immudb, an Open Source immutable database for systems and applications. Given the immutability of anything that is stored inside our immudb database, we think that it can be a valuable platform to store sensitive data important to current political or social discourse, so that data scientists can then get insight from data without fear of partisan or malevolent meddling with the core datasets. We started with the US federal government PPP dataset but we will add more in the future. Our service is free for all to use.
